A Cat a Day Keeps the Pain Away

Janurary 2012- August 2023

A cat can make a person ten times more happier. It is a known fact that once a cat cuddles on your lap, you automaticly stop moving. Cat enjoy your company so you should enjoy they're company too. Many of my artworks are featured with cats in one element of another. As a cat owner, I can say that my cat Eva has comforted me in the toughest of times. Whether she wants it or not you can't go wrong with cat hugs (unless they REALLY don't want to).
